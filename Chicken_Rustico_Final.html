Chicken Rustico

This Chicken Rustico recipe, inspired by Italian cuisine, is easy and pretty quick!

Takes around 35 minutes.

Instructions

Step 1 | Step 2 | Step 3 | Step 4
  1. Make sure to prep all of your ingredients first.
  2. Warm up one tablespoon of oil in a large nonstick skillet. Cook chicken for five minutes. Remove and set aside.
  3. Heat up another tablespoon of oil in the same skillet. Add the garlic and the mushrooms. Saut√© these, stirring for about five minutes or so. Stir in the broth, wine, and the mustard. Return the chicken to the skillet. Add your roma tomatoes and artichoke hearts to the mix. Bring it all to a boil and reduce the heat and cover. Cook for around ten minutes. Make sure that the chicken is no longer pink in the middle.
  4. Mix the cornstarch with one tablespoon of cold water. Stir this into the sauce to thicken the sauce. Bring it to a boil. Cook for about one minute, stirring every now and then. Serve it over some cooked orzo pasta.
