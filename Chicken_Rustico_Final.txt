   	        	<ol>
                	<li id="step1">
                    		PREPARE all ingredients
                    		<br>
                    		<img src="Chicken_Rustico_03.jpeg" alt="Prepared Ingredients" class="recipe-image">
                	</li>

                	<li id="step2">
                    		HEAT 1 tbsp. oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Cook chicken for 5 					minutes or until browned on both sides. Remove and set aside.
                    		<br>
                    		<img src="Chicken_Rustico_07.jpeg" alt="Cooked Chicken" class="recipe-image">
                	</li>

                	<li id="step3">
                    		HEAT 1 tbsp. oil in the same skillet until hot. Add garlic and mushrooms. Saut√© for 5 					minutes or until mushrooms are tender. Stir in broth, wine, and mustard. Return chicken to 				skillet. Add tomatoes and artichoke hearts. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low and 				cover. Cook for 10 minutes or until chicken is no longer pink in the center.
                    		<br>
                    		<img src="Chicken_Rustico_09.jpeg" alt="Covered Sauce" class="recipe-image">
                	</li>

                	<li id="step4">
                    		COMBINE cornstarch with 1 tbsp. cold water. Stir into the sauce. Heat to boiling. Cook, 				stirring, over high heat for about 1 minute or until sauce thickens. Serve with steamed 				zucchini or, if desired, cooked orzo pasta.
